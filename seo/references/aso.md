# ASO & Marketplace SEO Reference

Optimization for app stores (App Store, Google Play) and listing platforms (G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, Trustpilot).

---

## App Store Optimization (iOS)

### Ranking Factors

| Factor | Weight | Optimization |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| App name + subtitle keywords | High | Include primary keyword in name, secondary in subtitle |
| Hidden keyword field | High | 100 chars, comma-separated, no spaces after commas, no duplicates of name/subtitle |
| Ratings & review volume | High | Prompt happy users (after key achievement), respond to all reviews |
| Download velocity | High | Concentrated launch marketing, ASA campaigns |
| Engagement/retention | Medium | Onboarding quality, push notification strategy |
| Update frequency | Medium | Regular updates signal active development |
| In-app purchases | Low | Named descriptively for search discovery |

### Keyword Research

1. **Seed keywords** — core product terms, competitor names, use-case phrases
2. **Expand** — Apple Search Ads suggestions, competitor keyword analysis (tools: Sensor Tower, AppTweak, data.ai)
3. **Filter** — relevance × search volume × competition difficulty
4. **Distribute** — primary in name, secondary in subtitle, tertiary in keyword field
5. **Track** — weekly rank tracking, re-optimize monthly

### Listing Optimization

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|-------------|
| **Name** (30 chars) | Brand + primary keyword: "Todoist: To-Do List & Planner" |
| **Subtitle** (30 chars) | Secondary benefit keyword: "Organize work & life" |
| **Description** (4,000 chars) | Not indexed for search but influences conversion. Lead with value prop, bullet features, social proof |
| **Screenshots** (up to 10) | First 3 visible without scrolling. One benefit per screenshot with caption overlay |
| **Preview video** (15-30 sec) | Show the product in action, not a brand video. First 3 seconds are auto-played |
| **Promotional text** (170 chars) | Updatable without review. Use for seasonal offers, new features |
| **What's New** (4,000 chars) | Highlight user-facing changes. Not indexed but builds trust |

### Conversion Rate Optimization

| Element | Test | Impact |
|---------|------|--------|
| Icon | Color, style, elements | High (first thing users see) |
| Screenshots | Order, captions, style | High (persuasion sequence) |
| Preview video | With vs without, different hooks | Medium-High |
| Description | First 3 lines (above fold) | Medium |
| Ratings prompt timing | After achievement vs after time | Medium (review volume) |

## Google Play Store Optimization

### Key Differences from iOS

| Factor | App Store | Google Play |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Keyword source | Name + subtitle + hidden field | Name + short desc + full desc (all indexed) |
| Description indexing | Not indexed for search | Fully indexed for search |
| A/B testing | Custom Product Pages (limited) | Native Experiments (built-in) |
| Feature graphic | Not required | Required (1024x500) — prominent in listing |
| Review responses | Optional | Strongly recommended (impacts ranking) |

### Google Play Specifics

- **Short description** (80 chars): Indexed for search — treat as keyword-rich elevator pitch
- **Full description** (4,000 chars): Indexed — use natural keyword density (avoid stuffing), structure with line breaks
- **Feature graphic** (1024x500): Appears above screenshots in search results — treat as a billboard
- **Native A/B testing**: Use Store Listing Experiments to test graphics, descriptions, screenshots

## Marketplace SEO (G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, Trustpilot)

### G2 Optimization

| Factor | Impact | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Profile completeness** | High | Fill every field — company info, screenshots, integrations, pricing, alternatives |
| **Review volume** | High | Target 50+ reviews for category visibility. Run review campaigns quarterly |
| **Review recency** | High | Fresh reviews (last 90 days) weighted more. Maintain steady cadence |
| **Category selection** | High | Primary + secondary categories. "Best in Category" badges drive traffic |
| **Comparison pages** | Medium | G2 auto-generates comparison pages. Ensure your profile data is complete for accurate comparisons |
| **Response rate** | Medium | Respond to negative reviews within 48 hours |

### Capterra Optimization

| Factor | Impact | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Listing completeness** | High | Full description, screenshots, pricing, target market |
| **Review volume** | High | Target 25+ reviews for visibility |
| **Category presence** | High | List in all relevant categories |
| **Paid placement** | Available | Pay-per-click model, bid on categories |

### Product Hunt

| Factor | Impact | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Launch timing** | High | Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PST for full 24h cycle |
| **Maker profile** | High | Complete profile, comment engagement |
| **Product description** | High | Tagline (60 chars) + description (260 chars) — concise, benefit-driven |
| **Media assets** | High | Gallery images (1270x760), GIF/video preferred |
| **Community engagement** | High | Reply to every comment. Thank supporters. Be authentic |
| **Upvote velocity** | Critical | Organic outreach to network in first 4 hours |

### Review Management Strategy

**Generating reviews:**
1. Identify happy users (high NPS, power users, recent achievement)
2. Prompt at moment of delight (after success, not during task)
3. Make it frictionless — direct link to review form
4. Quarterly review campaigns (email sequence to active users)
5. Never incentivize reviews with rewards (violates most platform policies)

**Responding to reviews:**
- **Positive:** Thank specifically, highlight the feature they mentioned
- **Negative:** Acknowledge → explain (not excuse) → resolution → invite back
- **Response template for negative:**
  > "Thank you for the feedback, [Name]. We hear you on [specific issue]. [What we're doing about it / explanation]. If you'd like to discuss further, please reach out to [support email]. We'd love the chance to make this right."

## ASO Anti-Patterns

**Keyword stuffing** — Cramming keywords into the app name ("Best Todo List App Free Planner Organizer Tasks"). Rejected by Apple, penalized by Google.
INSTEAD: Brand + one primary keyword phrase.

**Ignoring screenshots** — Using raw app screenshots without captions or context. Users scroll past.
INSTEAD: One clear benefit per screenshot with caption overlay. First 3 screenshots are critical.

**Review farming** — Buying fake reviews. Detected and penalized by both platforms. Can result in app removal.
INSTEAD: Build genuine review velocity through in-app prompts at moments of delight.

**Static listings** — Never updating keywords, screenshots, or descriptions. Competitors optimize around you.
INSTEAD: Monthly keyword review, quarterly screenshot refresh, update description with new features.

**Ignoring marketplace profiles** — Active on App Store but no G2/Capterra presence. B2B buyers check review sites before purchasing.
INSTEAD: Complete profiles on all platforms where your ICP makes decisions. Maintain review velocity on each.
