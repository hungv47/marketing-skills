# 3D Angle Generation Framework

Generate angles by combining three dimensions: Hook Types × Awareness Levels × Emotional Triggers.

---

## Dimension 1: Hook Types

| Hook Type | Pattern | Example Starter |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Question** | Spark curiosity | "What if your [pain] isn't about [assumption]?" |
| **Bold claim** | Challenge belief | "[Common wisdom] is killing your [goal]" |
| **How-to** | Teach process | "How to [outcome] in [time] without [sacrifice]" |
| **Story** | Share experience | "I [action] and [unexpected result] happened" |
| **Data** | Lead with proof | "[X]% of [audience] are [surprising fact]" |
| **Sneak peek** | Behind scenes | "Here's what [insiders] actually do about [topic]" |
| **Contrarian** | Flip the script | "Stop trying to [common advice]. Do this instead." |
| **Analogy** | Make it click | "[Complex concept] is like [simple thing]" |

---

## Dimension 2: Awareness Level Fit

| Stage | Angle Goal | Language Pattern | Best Hooks |
|-------|------------|------------------|------------|
| Unaware | Surface problem | "Did you know..." | Question, Data |
| Problem Aware | Validate/agitate | "You're not alone..." | Story, Bold claim |
| Solution Aware | Differentiate | "There's a better way..." | How-to, Contrarian |
| Product Aware | Overcome objections | "You might think..." | Data, Sneak peek |
| Most Aware | Drive action | "Ready to..." | Story, How-to |

---

## Dimension 3: Emotional Triggers

| Trigger | Angle Frame | Language Cues |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| **Greed** | What they gain | "Get...", "Unlock...", "Access..." |
| **Fear** | What they lose | "Don't miss...", "Before it's too late..." |
| **Guilt** | What they should do | "You know you should...", "Stop ignoring..." |
| **Pride** | Who they become | "Join the top...", "Become the person who..." |
| **Anger** | What's holding them back | "Stop letting...", "Why [authority] is wrong..." |
| **Belonging** | Who they join | "The [community] secret...", "What [peers] are doing..." |

---

## Angle Generation Process

For each pillar, generate angles by combining dimensions:

| Pillar | Hook | Awareness | Trigger | Generated Angle |
|--------|------|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| [Pillar] | Question | Problem Aware | Anger | "Why does [pain] keep happening even when you [effort]?" |
| [Pillar] | Bold claim | Solution Aware | Pride | "[Common advice] is for beginners. Here's what experts do." |
| [Pillar] | How-to | Solution Aware | Greed | "How to [outcome] without [sacrifice] (step-by-step)" |
| [Pillar] | Story | Problem Aware | Belonging | "I thought I was the only one struggling with [pain]..." |

## Persona-Specific Angle Generation (Enhanced Mode)

When you have ICP personas, generate angles tailored to each persona's psychology.

### Step 1: Map Persona Context

| Persona | Top Emotional Drivers | Best Hooks | Awareness Stage | High-Weight Pillars |
|---------|----------------------|------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| [Persona A] | Pride > Fear > Greed | Question, How-to | Solution Aware | [Pillar 1, Pillar 3] |
| [Persona B] | Belonging > Guilt | Story, Bold claim | Problem Aware | [Pillar 2, Pillar 3] |
| [Persona C] | Greed > Anger | Data, Contrarian | Product Aware | [Pillar 1, Pillar 2] |

### Step 2: Generate Persona-Organized Angle Bank

```
**Persona: [Name]**
Primary Pillars: [High-weight pillars for this persona]
Top Triggers: [Ranked 1-3 with VoC evidence]
Awareness Stage: [Where they are]

- [Angle 1] (Hook: X, Trigger: Y)
  VoC Validation: "[Quote proving resonance]"

- [Angle 2] (Hook: X, Trigger: Y)
  VoC Validation: "[Quote proving resonance]"

- [Angle 3] (Hook: X, Trigger: Y)
  VoC Validation: "[Quote proving resonance]"
```

### Step 3: Journey-Based Objection Angles

Use decision journey barriers from ICP research to generate objection-handling angles:

| Journey Stage | Barrier | Persona Most Affected | Angle to Address |
|---------------|---------|----------------------|------------------|
| Consideration | "Too expensive" | [Persona B] | "Why [alternative] costs more long-term" |
| Consideration | "Another tool to learn" | [Persona A] | "How to start in 10 min, not 10 weeks" |
| Trial | "Team won't adopt" | [Persona C] | "Why teams actually use this (no forcing)" |
| Purchase | "Can't justify ROI" | [Persona B] | "The hidden cost of your current workaround" |

## Angle Prioritization

Score each angle 1-5 on:

| Criteria | Question to Ask |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Audience resonance** | Does this stop the scroll? |
| **Uniqueness** | Has this been said before? |
| **Provability** | Can we back it up? |
| **Channel fit** | Works on target channels? |
| **Repurpose potential** | Can this become 3+ pieces? |

Keep angles scoring 15+ (out of 25).
