## 9-Channel Framework

The complete channel map for growth. Evaluate every channel for relevance — don't default to digital-only.

### Channel Map

| # | Channel | Organic Tactics | Paid Tactics | Typical Role |
|---|---------|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| 1 | **Search engines/AEO** | SEO, AEO (AI search optimization) | Search ads (Google, Bing), Shopping ads | Acquisition (intent capture) |
| 2 | **Store/Listing platforms** | ASO (App Store/Play Store), G2/Capterra profiles, Product Hunt | Apple Search ads, Google Play ads, sponsored listings | Acquisition (marketplace discovery) |
| 3 | **Bounty/Info platforms** | Referral programs, affiliate networks | Incentive campaigns, bounty programs, paid referrals | Acquisition (incentivized) |
| 4 | **News** | Earned press coverage, contributed articles | Paid PR articles, guest-postings, press releases | Awareness + Trust |
| 5 | **Forums/Communities** | Threading (Reddit, VOZ, HN, Quora), community engagement | Paid ads (Reddit ads, forum sponsorships) | Awareness + Trust + Advocacy |
| 6 | **Social media** | Organic brand content, affiliate content, UGC (influencers/creators), Internal UGC (mass accounts, clippings) | Paid ads (Meta, X, LinkedIn, TikTok), boosted posts | Full funnel |
| 7 | **IRL** | Events, conferences, meetups, word of mouth | OOH (billboards, transit), Point of Sales materials, event sponsorships | Awareness + Relationship |
| 8 | **Mailbox** | Transactional emails, newsletters, onboarding sequences | Paid inbox ads (Gmail Sponsored Promotions) | Nurture + Conversion |
| 9 | **SMS** | Transactional SMS, order updates | Paid SMS campaigns, sponsorship SMS | Conversion + Re-engagement |

### Channel Selection by Growth Motion

| Channel | PLG Priority | SLG Priority | Why |
|---------|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Search engines/AEO | **High** — captures problem-aware searchers | **High** — captures intent | Both motions need search visibility |
| Store/Listing platforms | **High** — if product is app-based | **Medium** — for marketplace presence | PLG relies on self-serve discovery |
| Bounty/Info platforms | **Medium** — referral is PLG lever | **Medium** — affiliate extends reach | PLG: organic referral > paid incentive |
| News | **Low** — unless raising funding/launching | **Medium** — builds trust for enterprise | SLG needs credibility signals |
| Forums/Communities | **High** — builds community, advocacy | **Low** — unless ICP lives there | PLG: community IS the growth engine |
| Social media | **Medium** — organic + UGC | **High** — paid + outbound | SLG: paid social drives leads |
| IRL | **Low** — unless at events | **High** — conferences, demos, POS | SLG: relationship-driven sales |
| Mailbox | **High** — onboarding + activation | **High** — nurture + outbound | Both motions need email |
| SMS | **Low** — unless transactional | **Medium** — follow-ups, campaigns | SLG: direct response channel |

### Channel Evaluation Checklist

For each candidate channel, assess:

1. **ICP Habitat Match** — Does the ICP live here? (from icp-research habitat maps)
2. **Cost Model** — Paid, organic, or hybrid? What's the expected CAC contribution?
3. **Timeline to Impact** — How long until this channel delivers results? (Search: months. Paid ads: days. Community: quarters.)
4. **Resource Requirements** — Team size, content volume, tools, budget needed
5. **Measurability** — Can we track attribution? (Digital: high. OOH/IRL: low — needs proxy metrics)
6. **Scalability** — Can we increase spend/effort and get proportional returns?

## Content Classification: Searchable vs. Shareable

Every piece of content serves one of two demand modes. This is orthogonal to the pillar system — any pillar can produce both types.

| Type | Purpose | Characteristics | Distribution |
|------|---------|----------------|--------------|
| **Searchable** | Capture existing demand | Keyword-driven, answers known questions, evergreen, SEO-optimized | Blog, YouTube, Quora, SEO landing pages |
| **Shareable** | Create new demand | Novel insights, contrarian takes, original data, emotional hooks | Social, email, KOLs, communities |

**Why this matters:** Searchable content compounds over time (traffic grows). Shareable content spikes and decays (engagement-driven). A healthy content mix needs both — pure searchable builds slow, pure shareable requires constant production.

**Assignment rule:** For each angle in the Angle Bank, tag it as Searchable, Shareable, or Both. Then match to channels:
- Searchable angles → owned blog, YouTube, SEO-focused channels
- Shareable angles → social, community, KOL, email
- Both → prioritize for highest-effort production (these are your best content investments)

## Habitat-Informed Channel Strategy (Enhanced Mode)

When you have ICP personas with Audience Habitat Maps, use the habitat data to drive channel selection rather than guessing.

### Step 1: Import Habitat Data

Pull the Audience Habitat Map from ICP research for each persona:

| Persona | Channel Native | Channel Type | Channel Name | Density | Engagement Type | Habitat Role |
|---------|----------------|--------------|--------------|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| [Persona A] | [Platform] | [Community kind] | [Specific community] | [H/M/L] | [Lurker/Engager/Creator/Subscriber/Searcher] | [Discovery/Trust/Conversion] |

If habitat data is not available, fall back to Quick ICP Question 15 to capture it.

### Step 2: Habitat-to-Channel Strategy Translation

For each habitat entry, translate it into an IMC channel decision:

| Persona | Habitat (from ICP) | IMC Channel Decision | Rationale |
|---------|-------------------|---------------------|-----------|
| [Persona A] | LinkedIn → Industry groups → "SaaS Growth Leaders" (H density, Engager) | Owned: thought leadership posts | High-density habitat, audience engages actively — ideal for reach angles |
| [Persona A] | Reddit → Subreddits → r/startups (M density, Lurker) | Content: SEO-optimized articles | Persona lurks here — use for trust-building content, not engagement hooks |
| [Persona B] | Email → Newsletters → "Lenny's Newsletter" (H density, Subscriber) | Partner: sponsored placement | Persona subscribes to curated content — align angle with newsletter format |

**Translation rules:**
- **H density + Engager/Creator** → High-priority channel, allocate reach and engagement angles
- **H density + Lurker** → Use for awareness/trust content, don't expect engagement metrics
- **M density + Searcher** → SEO/content opportunity, optimize for discoverability
- **L density** → Deprioritize unless it's a high-trust conversion habitat

### Step 3: Habitat-Informed Channel Strategy per Persona

Map habitats to the discovery → trust → conversion funnel:

| Persona | Discovery (found via) | Trust-Building (validates at) | Conversion (acts at) |
|---------|----------------------|------------------------------|---------------------|
| [Persona A] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] |
| [Persona B] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] |

For each row, cite the specific habitat entry that justifies the channel choice.

**Platform behavior definitions:**
- **Lurker**: Consumes but rarely engages publicly
- **Engager**: Likes, comments, shares regularly
- **Creator**: Posts own content, active in discussions
- **Subscriber**: Prefers push content (email, notifications)
- **Searcher**: Uses search when problems arise (SEO opportunity)

## Angle-to-Channel Assignment

**Key principle: Each channel name gets one clear angle they own.**

Not: "KOL A posts about Direct Comparisons" (category)
But: "KOL A posts: Teams waste 5 hours/week on status updates" (specific angle)

Assign specific angles to specific channel names:

| Channel Native | Type | Channel Name | Assigned Angle | Why This Fit |
|----------------|------|--------------|----------------|--------------|
| X | Owned | @brand | [Core narrative angle] | Brand voice, daily consistency |
| X | Paid KOL | @kol_a | "[Specific angle with numbers]" | Their audience matches this angle |
| Instagram | Partner | @partner_b | [Product integration angle] | Credibility, their context |
| X | Ecosystem | @chain_official | [RT key announcements] | Amplification reach |
