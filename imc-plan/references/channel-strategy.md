# Channel Strategy

Detailed channel selection frameworks including fit matrices and habitat-informed strategy.

---

## Channel Types Reference (by role)

| Type | Role | Content Approach | Who Controls |
|------|------|------------------|--------------|
| **Owned** | Daily narrative, brand voice | Full messaging control, consistent cadence | You |
| **Partners** | Credibility, co-marketing | Standalone posts, mutual benefit, co-branded | Shared |
| **Ecosystem** | Amplification | RTs, quotes, light engagement | Them (you request) |
| **Paid KOLs** | Reach + specific angles | One hook per KOL, targeted messaging | Negotiated |
| **Earned** | Validation, PR | Press coverage, organic mentions | Them |

## Habitat-Informed Channel Strategy (Enhanced Mode)

When you have ICP personas with Audience Habitat Maps, use the habitat data to drive channel selection rather than guessing.

### Step 1: Import Habitat Data

Pull the Audience Habitat Map from ICP research for each persona:

| Persona | Channel Native | Channel Type | Channel Name | Density | Engagement Type | Habitat Role |
|---------|----------------|--------------|--------------|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| [Persona A] | [Platform] | [Community kind] | [Specific community] | [H/M/L] | [Lurker/Engager/Creator/Subscriber/Searcher] | [Discovery/Trust/Conversion] |

If habitat data is not available, fall back to Quick ICP Question 15 to capture it.

### Step 2: Habitat-to-Channel Strategy Translation

For each habitat entry, translate it into an IMC channel decision:

| Persona | Habitat (from ICP) | IMC Channel Decision | Rationale |
|---------|-------------------|---------------------|-----------|
| [Persona A] | LinkedIn → Industry groups → "SaaS Growth Leaders" (H density, Engager) | Owned: thought leadership posts | High-density habitat, audience engages actively — ideal for reach angles |
| [Persona A] | Reddit → Subreddits → r/startups (M density, Lurker) | Content: SEO-optimized articles | Persona lurks here — use for trust-building content, not engagement hooks |
| [Persona B] | Email → Newsletters → "Lenny's Newsletter" (H density, Subscriber) | Partner: sponsored placement | Persona subscribes to curated content — align angle with newsletter format |

**Translation rules:**
- **H density + Engager/Creator** → High-priority channel, allocate reach and engagement angles
- **H density + Lurker** → Use for awareness/trust content, don't expect engagement metrics
- **M density + Searcher** → SEO/content opportunity, optimize for discoverability
- **L density** → Deprioritize unless it's a high-trust conversion habitat

### Step 3: Habitat-Informed Channel Strategy per Persona

Map habitats to the discovery → trust → conversion funnel:

| Persona | Discovery (found via) | Trust-Building (validates at) | Conversion (acts at) |
|---------|----------------------|------------------------------|---------------------|
| [Persona A] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] |
| [Persona B] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] | [Habitat: Channel Name] |

For each row, cite the specific habitat entry that justifies the channel choice.

**Platform behavior definitions:**
- **Lurker**: Consumes but rarely engages publicly
- **Engager**: Likes, comments, shares regularly
- **Creator**: Posts own content, active in discussions
- **Subscriber**: Prefers push content (email, notifications)
- **Searcher**: Uses search when problems arise (SEO opportunity)

## Angle-to-Channel Assignment

**Key principle: Each channel name gets one clear angle they own.**

Not: "KOL A posts about Direct Comparisons" (category)
But: "KOL A posts: Teams waste 5 hours/week on status updates" (specific angle)

Assign specific angles to specific channel names:

| Channel Native | Type | Channel Name | Assigned Angle | Why This Fit |
|----------------|------|--------------|----------------|--------------|
| X | Owned | @brand | [Core narrative angle] | Brand voice, daily consistency |
| X | Paid KOL | @kol_a | "[Specific angle with numbers]" | Their audience matches this angle |
| Instagram | Partner | @partner_b | [Product integration angle] | Credibility, their context |
| X | Ecosystem | @chain_official | [RT key announcements] | Amplification reach |
